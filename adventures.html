Adventures




Have fun with us!

Join Kingsman Hotels on an exciting journey where thrill-seekers and nature lovers can partake in a variety of exhilarating experiences. Our hotels offer a wide variety of adventures catered to suit every traveler's passion, from tranquil nature walks and cultural excursions to heart-pounding outdoor escapades like trekking, wildlife safaris, and water sports. Enjoy the comfort and luxury for which Kingsman is renowned while exploring the uncharted territories and vibrant cultures of our locations.


Have fun hiking
^[Click/Hover]^

With Kingsman Hotels, go on the ultimate hiking adventure in Sinharaja's pristine rainforests. Our escorted hiking excursions take you right to the center of this UNESCO World Heritage site, where you can explore the lush vegetation, cascading waterfalls, and exotic wildlife.

Explore the Ocean
^[Click/Hover]^

With the exhilarating scuba diving excursions offered by Kingsman Hotels, explore the underwater wonders. You are invited to explore the fascinating world beneath the waves with the help of our expertly led underwater excursions. Meet majestic sea creatures, explore vibrant coral reefs teeming with exotic marine life, and take in the breathtaking beauty of the ocean's depths. Our scuba diving experiences cater to all skill levels, whether you are an experienced diver or a beginner, guaranteeing a safe and unforgettable journey into the underwater world.

Tiny Sea Friends
^[Click/Hover]^

Join Kingsman Hotels on a warm-hearted journey as you visit a turtle hatchery farm. Observe the magnificent sea turtles' journey from the nest to the ocean and support conservation efforts. Our guided tours offer a special chance to discover more about these threatened species, their life cycles, and the critical function hatcheries play in their preservation. Enjoy the satisfaction of releasing baby turtles into the ocean knowing that you contributed to their survival.
